Basic Sabzee

Description

This recipe is the foundation for making a gravy-based sabzee with the vegetable of your choice. For best flavor, grind your own spices with an electric coffee grinder or mortar and pestle.

Ingredients

  • 5–6 cups chopped vegetables (uniform size)
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Masala:
  • ¼ cup ghee or oil
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced/chopped
  • ¼ cup finely chopped fresh ginger
  • 5–6 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp crushed red chiles or cayenne
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2–3 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 2 tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a stir-fry pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onions, ginger, and garlic. Sauté about 10 minutes until soft and lightly browned.
  3. Make a pool of oil in the center, raise heat, add turmeric, chiles, and cumin. Sizzle for 30 seconds.
  4. Stir in remaining spices and cook another 30 seconds.
  5. Add tomatoes and salt. Cook 5–10 minutes, stirring, until a saucy masala forms. Add water if needed.
  6. Add vegetables, stir, and cook until heated through.
  7. Cover, lower heat, and cook until vegetables are soft. Stir in cilantro and adjust seasoning.
